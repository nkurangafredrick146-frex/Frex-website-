FREX researchers are beginning a new project focused on understanding viral replication and immune response.  
The goal is to evolve an **X‑System** that adapts to emerging pathogens and accelerates vaccine discovery.

## The Science
We are building computational models of viral proteins and simulating immune interactions using FREX SOS.

## Impact
This project aims to create a scalable vaccine discovery pipeline that can be applied across multiple diseases.
